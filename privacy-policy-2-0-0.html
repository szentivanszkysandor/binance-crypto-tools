Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 30, 2025

1. Overview

The “Binance – Crypto Tools & Alert” Chrome extension provides a unified, read-only dashboard for Binance Spot market data with live Price, RSI(14), 24h%, and an EMA Trend (20/50/200). It includes manual and automatic alerting and several built-in tools. The extension is independent and not affiliated with Binance; it does not request keys, logins or wallet access.

Version 2.0.0 adds:

  • Market Pulse — consolidated context cards from public APIs (Fear & Greed, mempool/fees, BTC/ETH funding & basis, breadth, total market cap).
  • Global Ticker — an optional TradingView ticker-tape widget (iframe) that shows macro indices/FX/commodities (DXY, US 2Y/10Y, VIX, S&P 500, WTI, Gold…); no programmatic TV API calls are made.
  • Auto Alerts 2.0 — locally evaluated RSI, EMA cross (20×50/200), Price×EMA (20/50/200), Volume Spike (open-bar gates: progress/body/persist), and 24h% alerts with global cooldown, per-indicator timeframes and optional closed-bar mode.
  • Offscreen audio & WS acceleration — offscreen document to play sounds and background WebSocket streaming for near real-time updates while the popup is closed.

All computations run locally in your browser. No personal data, identifiers or browsing history are collected or transmitted.

2. Permissions Used

  • storage — saves your configuration and small caches:
    • Selected Spot symbols/timeframes, table column toggles, layout (hide tools, hide prices, compact view), active tab.
    • Manual & auto alert definitions (thresholds, one-shot, hysteresis, min-repeat, cooldown, styles, mute/closed-bar, per-indicator TF lists).
    • Short-lived Market Pulse snapshots and recent klines for local indicator math.
  • notifications — shows system notifications when alerts fire; messages contain only market context (symbol, event) and no personal data.
  • alarms — wakes the Service Worker to refresh public data and evaluate alerts while the popup is closed.
  • offscreen — creates an audio-only offscreen page to play alert sounds; no mic/cam, no recording, destroyed after use.
  • Host permissions (read-only):
    • https://api.binance.com/* & https://fapi.binance.com/* — Spot/Futures public endpoints (prices/klines/24h, funding & basis).
    • https://stream.binance.com/* & https://fstream.binance.com/* — WebSocket streaming for prices/klines.
    • https://api.coingecko.com/*, https://api.alternative.me/*, https://mempool.space/* — public indices for Market Pulse.
    • TradingView widget is loaded as an iframe from https://s.tradingview.com/ (no programmatic API access).

2.1 Embedded TradingView widget

The Global Ticker block embeds an official TradingView price widget via <iframe> from s.tradingview.com. The extension does not inject identifiers, does not read cookies and does not call TradingView APIs. TradingView may apply its own cookies/telemetry under its policy on their domain. You can disable this block anytime in Options.

2.2 Clipboard Use

Clicking “Copy address” in the donation dialog writes the shown BNB (BEP20) address to your clipboard. The extension never reads clipboard contents or writes without your explicit click.

3. Data Storage and Use

  • User settings are saved in Chrome storage (sync and local). Short-lived caches (Market Pulse, klines, cooldown stamps) exist only to render UI and evaluate alerts and expire automatically.
  • No personal data is collected, processed or transmitted. No browsing history, no account credentials, no wallets.
  • Network use is limited to the public endpoints listed above (HTTPS or WebSocket). No tracking or analytics SDKs are embedded.

4. Binance API and WebSocket Data

  • All requests are read-only; no API keys or account access.
  • REST: prices/24h/klines/premium index; WS: price/kline streams for near real-time alerts.
  • Requests contain no personal identifiers; results are processed locally for indicators and alerts.

5. Market Pulse sources

  • Fear & Greed — api.alternative.me/fng
  • BTC mempool & fees — mempool.space/api/…
  • Funding & basis (BTC/ETH) — fapi.binance.com/fapi/v1/premiumIndex
  • 24h breadth — api.binance.com/api/v3/ticker/24hr
  • Global market cap & dominance — api.coingecko.com/api/v3/global

Responses are cached in Chrome storage with short TTLs. No user data is attached to these requests.

6. Auto Alerts (local)

  • RSI, EMA cross (20×50/200), Price×EMA (20/50/200), Volume Spike (open-bar gates: progress/body/persist) and 24h%.
  • Global cooldown, optional closed-bar mode, per-indicator timeframe lists; EMA fine filters (separation/hold/full).
  • On trigger, a browser notification is shown and (if enabled) a short sound is played via offscreen audio.

7. Security & No Tracking

  • No telemetry, analytics or advertising SDKs. No fingerprinting.
  • No page content scripts; the extension does not read pages or tabs.
  • All external requests use HTTPS; WebSockets connect to Binance’s official hosts only.
  • The offscreen document is ephemeral and audio-only.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Settings persist until you reset them in Options or remove the extension.
  • Short-lived caches auto-expire (minutes) or are cleared on reset/uninstall.
  • Removing the extension deletes its Chrome storage data.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Binance public APIs — prices/klines/24h and premium index; no account endpoints.
  • Coingecko / Alternative.me / Mempool.space — public index feeds for Market Pulse.
  • TradingView — iframe widget for macro prices (no direct API calls by the extension; see TradingView’s own policy).
  • Donations — optional external links (e.g., PayPal, Binance address); the extension does not collect payment data.

10. Incognito Mode

Behavior and privacy are identical to normal mode; no additional collection occurs.

11. Children’s Privacy

This extension is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

12. No Financial Advice

All data and signals are informational/educational only and do not constitute investment or trading advice.

13. Data Practices Summary

  • Data collected: None (only local settings/caches in Chrome storage).
  • Data shared/sold: None.
  • Network calls: Anonymous requests to public Binance & index APIs; optional TradingView iframe for display only.
