Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

1. Overview

The “Binance – Crypto Tools” Chrome extension provides a simple launcher for crypto dashboards: a pinned Binance link and a Tools section with buttons that open your GitHub‑hosted mini‑apps (e.g. Order Book, Trades, Volume, Price/EMA, Pattern Scanner, Macro/Crypto Risk). The extension itself does not analyze, collect, transmit, or sell personal data. It only opens links and stores your custom buttons locally.

This is an independent, unofficial project and is not affiliated with Binance. The dashboards are for educational and informational use only and do not constitute financial advice.

2. Permissions Used

  • storage: Save your custom links (label + URL) in chrome.storage.sync so they can optionally sync with your Google account if Chrome Sync is enabled.
  • tabs: Open the selected links in a new browser tab.

No content scripts, host permissions, or analytics are used by this extension.

3. Data Storage and Use

  • Custom links: If you add buttons, their labels and URLs are stored in chrome.storage.sync.
  • No personal data: The extension does not collect names, emails, wallets, balances, or browsing history.
  • No transmission: Settings remain in Chrome storage; nothing is sent to external servers by the extension.

4. Third‑Party Services

  • GitHub Pages: Your dashboards are hosted by GitHub Pages or other sites you configure. Their use is governed by those sites’ terms and privacy policies.
  • Binance APIs: The dashboards you open may fetch market data directly from Binance endpoints. Requests are made by the webpage you open, not by the extension.
  • Donation link: The extension shows a static “Donation” link that opens your external payment page (e.g., PayPal). Payments are processed by that provider; this extension does not process or store payment data.

5. No Tracking or Analytics

  • No usage analytics or telemetry.
  • No cookies or unique identifiers are set by the extension.
  • No page contents are read or stored.

6. Incognito Mode

The extension can run in Incognito only if you enable it in Chrome. Behavior is the same as in regular mode; custom links are stored by Chrome in the appropriate profile storage and are not transmitted by the extension.

7. Security

All extension settings stay in your browser’s local/sync storage. There are no backend servers operated by this extension, and no passwords or payment information are handled by the extension.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: Custom links persist until you change or remove them.
  • Manage: Use the Options page to add, reorder, or delete your custom links.
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension deletes its local and sync storage in Chrome.

9. Changes

As features evolve, this policy may be updated. The latest version will be published via the Chrome Web Store listing or the linked repository.

10. Contact

For support or questions, contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing or the project’s GitHub repository.

This extension is designed to comply with the Chrome Web Store privacy requirements.
